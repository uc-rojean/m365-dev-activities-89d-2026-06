# Naming Standards

## Purpose

This document defines the high-level naming standards intended to support consistency, organization, and long-term maintainability across the Microsoft 365 environment.

The goal is to provide a repeatable and scalable approach for organizing files, folders, documentation, and future collaboration resources.

## Scope

Areas covered include:

- File naming concepts
- Folder naming concepts
- Consistency guidelines
- Organizational reference standards
- Future alignment considerations

Detailed standards may evolve as development, testing, and operational requirements mature.

## Maintenance

This document is maintained as a standards reference.

Updates are performed whenever naming conventions, organizational standards, or governance requirements change.

This document does **not** require daily, weekly, weekend, holiday, or leave-period updates.

## Naming Structure Guidelines

### Organizational Folders

Top-level organizational folders may use numeric prefixes to establish consistent ordering and long-term structure.

Examples:

- 000_FOUNDATION
- 001_INVESTMENT
- 002_CLOUD_STORAGE_SOLUTIONS

### Topic-Based Folders

Topic folders do not require numeric prefixes where ordering is not important.

Examples:

- MICROSOFT
- FOREX
- MEDICAL
- CREDIT_CARD

### Documents

Documents may use a structured naming approach that includes:

- Topic identifier
- Document name
- Version number

Examples:

- 000_Road_Map_v1.0.docx
- 001_Phase_1_Recap_v2.0.docx

The objective is to support consistency, readability, and future scalability without introducing unnecessary complexity.
