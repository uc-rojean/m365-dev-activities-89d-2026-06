# Reconstruction Log

## Purpose

This document tracks major reconstruction milestones, planning decisions, and development readiness activities associated with the Microsoft 365 Developer Sandbox renewal cycle.

The log serves as a consolidated record of significant planning and reconstruction progress while maintaining separation from confidential business information.

## Scope

This document may include:

- Reconstruction milestones
- Planning activities
- Readiness assessments
- Development preparation activities
- Major project decisions

Sensitive operational details, business plans, and implementation-specific information are intentionally excluded from the public repository.

## Maintenance

This document is considered an active planning log.

Updates should be made whenever significant reconstruction activities, planning milestones, or readiness events occur.

Unlike reference documents, this file may receive periodic updates throughout the project lifecycle as reconstruction efforts progress.

This document does not require daily updates but should be reviewed during major development activities and planning milestones.
